## Communication method

According to the direction of information transmission on the transmission line, it is divided into the following three communication methods:

- Simplex communication: one-way transmission
- Half-duplex communication: two-way alternating transmission
- Full-duplex communication: simultaneous transmission in both directions

## Bandpass modulation

Analog signals are continuous signals, and digital signals are discrete signals. Bandpass modulation converts digital signals into analog signals.
